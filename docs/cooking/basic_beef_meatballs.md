# Basic Beef Meatballs

## Ingredients

| Ingredient               | Quantity   |
| ------------------------ | ---------- |
| Ground beef              | 1 lb (450g) |
| Garlic (minced)          | 2-3 cloves |
| Egg                      | 1 large    |
| Salt                     | To taste   |
| Black pepper             | To taste   |
| Paprika                  | Optional   |
| Dried herbs (oregano/thyme) | Optional |
| Red pepper flakes        | Optional   |

## Directions

1. **Prep:** Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
2. **Mix:** In a bowl, combine ground beef, minced garlic, egg, and your chosen seasonings. Mix gently until just combined — don't overmix or they'll be tough.
3. **Form:** Roll into 1.5-inch meatballs (you'll get about 12-16 depending on size).
4. **Bake:** Place on baking sheet, leaving space between them. Bake 18-22 minutes until browned and cooked through (internal temp 160°F/71°C).

## Tips

- For crispier exterior: broil last 2-3 minutes
- For juicier meatballs: use higher fat beef (80/20)
- Season generously — they need it without other ingredients
