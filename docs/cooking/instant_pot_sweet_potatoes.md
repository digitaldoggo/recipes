# Instant Pot Sweet Potatoes

Velvety, fluffy sweet potatoes cooked under pressure — a fraction of the time vs. baking in the oven. Set it and forget it for busy weeknights or meal prep.

**Source:** [Preppy Kitchen](https://preppykitchen.com/instant-pot-sweet-potatoes/)

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| ---------- | -------- |
| Sweet Potatoes (same size) | 2–4, scrubbed clean |
| Cool Water | 1 cup (240mL) |

**Servings:** 2–4 · **Prep:** 10 min · **Cook:** 20–45 min (by size) + 10 min natural release

## Directions

1. Scrub sweet potatoes thoroughly and prick the top of each one a few times with a fork (3–4 pokes).
2. Place a trivet in the bottom of the Instant Pot. Arrange whole sweet potatoes in a single layer on the trivet.
3. Pour in 1 cup of water.
4. Secure the lid, make sure steam valve is in **Sealed** position. Cook at high pressure for time based on size (see below).
5. Allow natural pressure release for **10 minutes**, then manually release any remaining pressure.
6. Carefully remove with tongs. Serve hot or let cool and refrigerate.

## Cook Times by Size

| Size | Weight / Thickness | Pressure Time |
| ---- | ------------------ | ------------- |
| Small | 6–8 oz, ~2" thick | 20 minutes |
| Medium | 9–14 oz, ~3" thick | 35 minutes |
| Large | 15+ oz, ~4" thick | 45 minutes |

> **Note:** Add ~20–25 min for the Instant Pot to come up to pressure. Total time from start: ~40–70 min depending on size.

## Pro Tips

- **Same size matters.** Pick potatoes close in shape and width so they cook evenly.
- **Pierce them!** Steam needs a way out — 3–4 fork pricks per potato. Skipping this can cause explosions.
- **Skin will split.** Unlike baked potatoes, the skin gets soft and cracks open during pressure cooking. That's normal.
- **Not done?** Return to high pressure for an additional 5 minutes — it comes back to pressure fast.
- **Overcooking isn't bad** with sweet potatoes — they get very soft without going grainy (great for mashing).

## How To Serve

| Style | Toppings |
| ----- | -------- |
| Simple side | Butter, salt, pepper |
| Sweet | Honey butter + cinnamon + maple syrup + brown sugar sprinkle |
| Tex-Mex main | Instant Pot pulled pork, diced avocado, salsa, black beans |

## Leftovers & Storage

| Method | Details |
| ------ | ------- |
| Fridge | Airtight container, up to 1 week (peel skin first if using in recipes) |
| Freeze | Wrap individually in plastic/foil, freezer bag or container, up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in fridge, reheat in microwave. |

## Uses for Cooked Sweet Potatoes

- Mashed sweet potatoes
- Sweet potato biscuits (remove skin first)
- Sweet potato gnocchi
- Sweet potato pie
- Sweet potato casserole
