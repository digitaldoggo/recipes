# Banana Bread

Rich, moist banana bread with a soft crumb and hint of cinnamon. The best way to use up overripe bananas.

**Source:** [Preppy Kitchen](https://preppykitchen.com/banana-bread/)

## Ingredients

| Ingredient              | Quantity       |
| ----------------------- | -------------- |
| All-Purpose Flour       | 1¾ cups (210g) |
| Baking Soda             | 1 teaspoon     |
| Salt                    | 1 teaspoon     |
| Ground Cinnamon         | ½ teaspoon     |
| Light or Dark Brown Sugar | ¾ cup (165g) |
| Unsalted Butter (room temp, softened) | ½ cup (113g) |
| Eggs                    | 2 large        |
| Mashed Ripe Bananas     | 1¾ cups (430g, ~4-5 bananas) |
| Vanilla Extract         | 1 teaspoon     |

**Servings:** 1 loaf (~10 slices) · **Prep:** 10 min · **Cook:** 50–60 min · **Cool:** 20 min

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F**. Grease a 9×5-inch loaf pan with butter or baking spray.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Set aside.
3. In a large mixing bowl (or stand mixer with paddle attachment), beat brown sugar and softened butter on high speed until light and fluffy (~3 minutes).
4. Beat in eggs one at a time, scraping down the bowl between each. Mix in vanilla extract.
5. Add mashed bananas and mix briefly — leave some chunks for texture.
6. Fold dry ingredients into wet ingredients **until just combined** (don't overmix!).
7. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
8. Bake 50–60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
9. Cool in pan 20 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.

## Pro Tips

- **Ripe bananas are key.** Peels should be mostly or completely brown with mushy flesh. Underripe = bland bread.
- **Measure flour correctly.** Use a kitchen scale, or fluff → spoon → level method. Too much flour = dry crumbly bread.
- **Softened butter.** Set out ~1 hour before baking. Creaming softened butter gives a springy, light loaf.
- **Room-temp eggs.** They bind better than cold eggs straight from the fridge.
- **Leave chunks in bananas.** A few banana pieces per bite is nice — don't puree them completely.

## Variations

| Variation | Details |
| --------- | ------- |
| Banana nut bread | Add 1 cup toasted walnuts or pecans |
| Dried fruit | Add 1 cup raisins |
| Chocolate chip | Add ½ cup regular or mini chocolate chips |
| Sour cream tang | Replace 1 banana with ¼ cup sour cream or Greek yogurt (keeps it moist) |
| Frosting top | Cream cheese frosting, chocolate ganache, or chocolate cream cheese frosting |
| Extra spices | Add ½ tsp allspice, ¼ tsp cloves, ¼ tsp cardamom, or ¼ tsp nutmeg |

## Storage & Freezing

| Method | Details |
| ------ | ------- |
| Room temp | Airtight container, ~4 days |
| Fridge | Tightly wrapped, up to 7 days (may dry slightly) |
| Freeze | Wrap whole loaf or individual slices in plastic + freezer bag, up to 4 months. Thaw overnight in fridge or a few hours on counter. |
